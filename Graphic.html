The Tools I use

Figma

Figma is a collaborative design tool that enables real-time teamwork on interface design and prototyping. Its cloud-based platform allows seamless integration and communication among team members.

Illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is a vector graphics editor known for its precision and versatility in creating scalable designs. It offers a wide range of tools for detailed illustrations and professional-grade artwork.

Canva

Canva is an easy-to-use graphic design tool with a drag-and-drop interface, perfect for creating visually appealing content quickly. It provides a variety of templates and design elements to simplify the design process.
